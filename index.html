Hello, I'm Isabelly (or Sunny, artistically speaking), a professional deeply passionate about creating magic and telling stories for all ages for over 8 years.

I started at 15 years old as a live character performer at children's parties — and that's when I discovered something that changed my life: I was born to enchant. From that moment on, I truly immersed myself in this world. I've worked in hospitality, birthday parties, schools, camps, tourism and large-scale events — always with the same purpose: turning simple moments into memories that last forever.

I strongly believe in my calling. I believe in contagious energy, in attentive listening, in genuine care and in paying attention to every small detail. Whether it's getting messy during playtime, organizing a treasure hunt, dancing until we're out of breath or even playing video games with the kids — I genuinely love what I do, and you can see it.

At the same time, I carry the responsibility and professionalism the industry requires. I have worked in high-standard service environments, participated in organizing important events, represented tourism initiatives in my city and even delivered lectures in the field.

I am exactly what you are looking for: someone who knows how to have fun with purpose, who understands that play is serious work, and who brings responsibility, sparkle in her eyes and true passion into everything she does.
